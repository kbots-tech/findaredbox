Welcome to the Redbox Database

This project is dedicated to preserving and exploring the history of Redbox kiosks, their locations, and the products they once offered. Whether you're looking for historical data about a specific kiosk, curious about the movies and games that were available, or simply interested in learning about the network, this is the place for you.

The database is a collaborative effort, built from community contributions and carefully maintained to ensure its accuracy and usefulness. Special thanks to the Redbox Tinkering community for their invaluable help and resources.

Specific thanks to, Brian Walczak (@briannw) for the geocoded data, @codeHusky for their json data of the kiosks and @puyo for their product dump json.
